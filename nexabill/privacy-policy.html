Privacy Policy for NexaBill

Effective Date: May 6, 2025

NexaBill (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We collect your name, email, phone number, and role (customer, cashier, admin) during sign-up.
  • Billing Details: We store product data, transaction summaries, and verification statuses to provide billing services.
  • Device Information: Firebase and analytics tools may collect device and usage data to improve performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate users via Firebase
  • To generate, verify, and store digital bills
  • To send OTPs and enable secure payment
  • To improve service quality using aggregated usage analytics

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To trusted services like Firebase, Supabase, and Razorpay strictly for app functionality

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and secure protocols to protect your data in transit and at rest.
